The Wheelchair for those who still believe

COGY is a revolutionary wheelchair can give hope to those with disabilities or those who have suffered injuries.

COGY wheelchairs are specially designed so that even the slightest push on the pedal can make the wheelchair go. This then can stimulate a reaction from pattern generator circuits that may still be preserved in the spine, allowing for movement in both legs.

Designed with safety in mind

Both front and rear wheels have brakes installed, allowing for safer use.

Three additional caster wheels are installed to supplement the wheelchair's stability.

Convenience

COGY can navigate tight spaces, with a control lever that allows you to turn on a dime.

For easy access, the armrests can be folded back, and the wheels can even be removed.
